Organizing Event

I dont't have any experience about working part time or anything else.
But I have one unforgettable experience that I will never forget.
During my last semester in diploma, me and my classmates need to
arrange an event for our class. After several discussion with our lecturer,
we decide to arranged a dinner event. We named it as "OM Alumnight 2019".
The reasons why we choose the name because, we were the last batch of
"Office Management and Technology in Collaboration with UiTM" at KPTM Alor Setar.

This event really bring back all the memories of our alumni.
We invited all the alumni from the first batch until the last batch.
It was really hard to organized this event. But we managed to do so
because of our teamwork.I gain many experience during organizing this event.
One of it is, I look for sponsorship for our event. It was really hard for me
to communicate with strangers but I managed to do that with the help of my friends.

I also challenged myself a new thing that I've never done before.
My duty during this event is as a promotion bureau. This was really a
new experience for me because I have to make a design for our
class logo, invitation card and the backdrop.
It really hard for me at first because I dont have any experience in designing.
But I managed to do so and learning a new thing. Because of this event,
now I have experienced in designing eventhough it just for one semester.

I put some of our memories here since this is my first experience in organizing an event.
